# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming, safe, and respectful experience for everyone participating in this project.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent and discuss technical issues with evidence.
- Accept feedback and collaborate toward solutions.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or exclusionary behavior.
- Publishing private information without permission.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards and taking fair corrective action when needed.

## Reporting

Please report behavior issues through the security and contact channels listed in [SECURITY.md](./SECURITY.md).
